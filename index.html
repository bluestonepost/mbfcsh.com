About the Book

MBFC+SH is built around a simple idea: better decisions should create more value, improve quality, increase speed, reduce cost, lower risk, and make work better for the people involved.

The book offers a practical framework for evaluating choices, improving operations, reducing frustration, and helping teams think more clearly about tradeoffs.

The Framework

More Increase useful output, value, capacity, or opportunity.
Better Improve quality, consistency, usefulness, and customer outcomes.
Faster Reduce delays, friction, waiting, and unnecessary steps.
Cheaper Lower waste, rework, avoidable expense, and total cost.
Safer Reduce risk, errors, exposure, and unintended consequences.
Happier Improve trust, morale, clarity, and the human experience of work.

Who This Book Is For

This book is for leaders, managers, operators, business owners, and anyone responsible for making practical decisions in the real world.

It is especially useful for people who want simple tools for improving performance without making work more complicated than it needs to be.

Practical Tools Included

MBFC+SH includes decision tools, review questions, worksheets, and audit prompts designed to help readers apply the framework immediately.

About the Author

Daniel Nichols is an operations leader with experience in manufacturing, supply chain, process improvement, and practical business decision-making. MBFC+SH reflects a hands-on approach to improving work by focusing on value, clarity, accountability, and better outcomes.
